Extremely Fast and Simple WebGL Motion Detector to Rotate 3D Graphic

by Markus Sprunck

In this experiment the browser captures the video signal and compares subsequent frames to detect motion of the user. With this signal the program finds the viewpoint and rotates the 3D cube. The algorithm is very simple, robust and extremely fast compared with classic face detection approaches. Many thanks to Pierre-Loic Doulcet for his inspiring chrome experiment Pixelate Yourself. Just allow this site to access your camera and move your head. The implementation is based on Three.js (r73). You may read more and get the source code here.
